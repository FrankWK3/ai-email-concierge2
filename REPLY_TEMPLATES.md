# Reply Templates

## Acknowledgment (General)
"Thanks for reaching out. I appreciate the update and will review this shortly. I’ll follow up if I have any questions."

## Warm Personal Reply
"Thanks so much for the note. I really appreciate you sharing this, and I hope everything is going well on your end."

## Scheduling (Open)
"Happy to connect. I’m generally available [days/times]. Let me know what works best for you."

## Scheduling (Confirm)
"That time works for me. I’ve added it to my calendar—looking forward to it."

## Holding Pattern
"Thanks for the update. I’ll keep an eye out for the follow-up and appreciate you letting me know."
